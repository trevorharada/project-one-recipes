Tequila Chili

  • Home

Description

The estimated prep time for this dish is about fifteen minutes and the estimated cook time is about thirty minutes. This recipe makes four servings of tequila chili. The end product should be a hearty, salty, savory, and spicy chili.

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can of Italian-styled stewed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of light red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 (8 ounce) can of tomatoe sauce
  • 1.5 fluid ounces of tequila
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1.5 tablespoons of ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons of cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium heat, breaking it up with a spoon while it cooks
  2. Drain the excess oil from the meat
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, kidney beans, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, tequila, chili powder, cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper
  4. Simmer 20 minutes then serve
